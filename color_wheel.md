# Color Wheel 

###Primary Colors

- Red 
- Blue
- Yellow

###Secundary Colors

- Orange
- Violet 
- Green

###Tertiary Colors

- Yellow-Orange
- Red-Orange
- Red-Violet
- Violet-Blue
- Blue-Green
- Yellow-Green

###Tint

- Mixing color with White
- Lighter than original color


###Shade

- Mixing color with Black
- Darker than original color

###Tone

- Mixing color with Grey
- Less saturated than original color
